Pizza:

History of pizza:

Pizza has a long and interesting history, with roots tracing back to ancient civilizations:

  • Ancient Beginnings:

Egypt & Greece: The concept of flatbreads topped with various ingredients dates back thousands of years. Egyptians are believed to have made a type of flatbread that was cooked on stones and topped with herbs and oil. The Greeks also had a similar flatbread dish known as "plakous," which was topped with ingredients like cheese, garlic, and onions.

Ingredients for Pizza:

  1. Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  2. Tomato sauce (pizza sauce or marinara sauce)
  3. Shredded mozzarella cheese
  4. Toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, onions, etc.)
  5. Olive oil (for brushing the crust)
  6. Dried oregano or basil (optional for extra flavor)

Steps to Make Pizza:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, then sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  3. Add your favorite toppings (like pepperoni, veggies, etc.).
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted. Slice and enjoy!
