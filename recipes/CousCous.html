CousCous:

What's the Couscous?

Couscous is a traditional Berber dish of semolina(granules of durum wheat)

which is cooked by steaming.

List of ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Carrot (optional)
  • Tomato sauce (Very important)

Instructions for making the CousCous:

  • In a pot, bring 1 cup of water (or broth) to a boil. Add a pinch of salt and the olive oil or butter.
  • once the water's boiling, remove the pot from heat. Pour in the 1 cup of couscous, give it a quick stir to spread it evenly.
  • Cover the pot with a lid and let it sit for about 5 minutes. The couscous will absorb all the liquid.
  • After 5 minutes, uncover it and use a fork to fluff the couscous break up any clumps and make it light and airy
  • Serve the meat, tomato sauce, eggs, and carrots on top of the couscous!
