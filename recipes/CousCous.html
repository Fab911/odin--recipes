CousCous:

What's the Couscous?

Couscous is a traditional Berber dish of semolina(granules of durum wheat)

which is cooked by steaming.

List of ingredients:

  1. Meat
  2. Potatoes
  3. Eggs
  4. Carrot (optional)
  5. Tomato sauce (Very important)

Instructions for making the CousCous:

  1. In a pot, bring 1 cup of water (or broth) to a boil. Add a pinch of salt and the olive oil or butter.
  2. once the water's boiling, remove the pot from heat. Pour in the 1 cup of couscous, give it a quick stir to spread it evenly.
  3. Cover the pot with a lid and let it sit for about 5 minutes. The couscous will absorb all the liquid.
  4. After 5 minutes, uncover it and use a fork to fluff the couscous break up any clumps and make it light and airy
  5. Serve the meat, tomato sauce, eggs, and carrots on top of the couscous!
